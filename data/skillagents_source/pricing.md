# SkillAgents AI Pricing

SkillAgents AI runs a live, cohort-based course called "AI Coding for Product Managers". The course is six weeks long with weekly Zoom sessions, a private Slack community, and office hours with Rajesh P. Five plans are available. Pick the one that matches how you plan to learn.

## Free Trial

Price: $0. Length: seven days. No credit card required.

You get the first module of the current cohort, including the recorded lectures and the week one homework. This plan exists so you can see the teaching style before you pay. When the seven days end, access stops unless you upgrade.

## Student

Price: $499, one-time payment.

The Student plan covers a single cohort from start to finish. You get the weekly live Zoom sessions, the Slack community for the duration of the cohort, and all homework reviews. It does not include group mentorship, does not carry over to future cohorts, and does not include team features. It is the right plan for an individual PM who wants to take the course once and move on.

## Pro Annual

Price: $1,499 per year.

Pro Annual includes access to every cohort that runs during your twelve months. You can retake the course, jump into the next cohort for a refresher, or cherry-pick sessions from different cohorts. Pro Annual also includes one group mentorship session per month with Rajesh and a certificate of completion you can share on LinkedIn. It is the right plan for PMs who want to build an ongoing practice, not just a one-time class.

## Team

Price: $4,999 per year for up to five seats.

Team includes everything in Pro Annual for each seat. On top of that, your team gets a private Slack channel inside the SkillAgents community and a quarterly manager debrief where Rajesh walks the manager through what the team learned and where they are getting stuck. It is the right plan for product managers at the same company who want to level up together.

## Enterprise

Price: custom.

Enterprise is for organizations with more than five product managers. It includes unlimited seats, dedicated onboarding, SSO, and a custom curriculum tailored to your stack and your product areas. Contact sales@skillagents.ai for a quote.

For reference, Q3 2026 revenue totaled $2.4 million across three segments: $1.1M from Pro Annual, $0.9M from Team, and $0.4M from Student one-time purchases.
