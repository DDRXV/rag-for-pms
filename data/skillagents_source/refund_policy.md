# SkillAgents AI Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## 1. Overview

This policy explains how refunds work for every SkillAgents AI plan. It applies to all customers who purchased directly from skillagents.ai. If you bought access through a reseller or marketplace, refer to that marketplace's terms instead. Questions about your specific order should go to support@skillagents.ai with the order ID from your receipt.

## 2. Free Trial

The Free Trial gives you the first module of the "AI Coding for Product Managers" cohort for seven days, with no credit card required. Because the Free Trial is free, it is not refundable. When the seven days end, you lose access unless you upgrade to a paid plan.

## 3. Student Plan

The Student plan is a $499 one-time payment for access to a single cohort. It is refundable in full up until the moment you view the first module. Once the first module is viewed, the Student plan is non-refundable. This rule is enforced automatically based on video playback telemetry, not on self-reporting.

## 4. Pro Annual

The Pro Annual plan is $1,499 per year and includes access to every cohort for twelve months, one group mentorship session per month, and a certificate of completion.

### 4.1 Cancellation process

You can cancel Pro Annual at any time from the billing page. Cancellation stops future renewals right away. It does not by itself issue a refund. To receive money back, you must also submit a refund request as described in Section 7.

### 4.2 Pro-rated refund calculation

Pro Annual refunds are calculated as a pro-rated refund minus two months of service used. The two months are treated as consumed regardless of actual usage. A $50 processing fee is deducted from every refund.

Worked example. A customer pays $1,499 and cancels five months into the year. Seven months are refundable. The pro-rated amount is $1,499 multiplied by seven divided by twelve, which is $874.42. After the $50 processing fee, the customer receives $824.42.

## 5. Team Plan

The Team plan is $4,999 per year for up to five seats. It is refundable in full before the first cohort kickoff for that team. After the first cohort kickoff, the Team plan is non-refundable for the remainder of the year.

## 6. Enterprise

Enterprise refunds are governed by the signed master services agreement. Contact your account manager for the terms that apply to your contract.

## 7. How to request a refund

Send an email to support@skillagents.ai with your order ID and the reason for the request. The billing team responds within two business days. Approved refunds land back on the original payment method within seven to ten business days.
