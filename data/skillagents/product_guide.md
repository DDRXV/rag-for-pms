# SkillAgents AI Product Guide

Welcome to SkillAgents AI. This guide explains how the "AI Coding for Product Managers" cohort actually runs, so you know what to expect in your first week and how to get the most out of the six weeks.

## How a cohort works

Each cohort lasts six weeks. A new cohort kicks off on the first Monday of every month. You stay with the same group of PMs for the whole six weeks, which is the point. You learn faster when you have classmates who are stuck on the same things you are.

The weekly schedule is fixed:

- Monday, 8:00 AM Pacific: live Zoom session with Rajesh. Ninety minutes. Half lecture, half live coding. Recorded.
- Tuesday through Thursday: homework. Budget two to three hours across the week.
- Friday, 9:00 AM Pacific: office hours with Rajesh. Sixty minutes. Bring your screen, bring your bugs.
- Slack: always on. Post questions any time.

## How to enroll

Enrollment opens two weeks before each cohort starts. Go to skillagents.ai, pick a plan, and complete checkout. You will receive a welcome email within five minutes with your Slack invite, your Zoom links for all six Monday sessions, and the link to the learning portal.

If you bought the Free Trial, you get the first module only. If you upgrade later, your progress carries over.

## How to access recordings

Every Monday session is recorded and posted to the learning portal by 6:00 PM Pacific the same day. Recordings stay available for the length of your plan. Student plan recordings are available for the six weeks of your cohort. Pro Annual recordings are available for the full twelve months across every cohort you have access to.

## How mentorship works

Pro Annual includes one group mentorship session per month. These are small-group calls, capped at eight PMs, where you bring a real problem from your job and Rajesh works through it with you on the call. You book your slot from the mentorship page in the portal. The Student plan does not include mentorship. Enterprise plans can add one-on-one mentorship as a contract line item.

## How the certificate works

The certificate of completion is issued at the end of any cohort where you submit at least four of the six weekly homework assignments. It is a PDF with a verification link you can add to your LinkedIn profile. Student and Pro Annual both qualify. Free Trial does not, because the trial only covers one module.

## How Team features work

Team plans get a private Slack channel separate from the main cohort Slack. Your team posts there without the rest of the cohort seeing it, which matters when you are discussing internal projects. Every quarter, Rajesh runs a sixty-minute debrief with the team's manager. The debrief covers what the team completed, where they struggled, and what to work on next quarter.

## What happens if you miss a session

Missing a Monday session is fine. Watch the recording, post questions in Slack, and bring anything you did not understand to Friday office hours. The homework is designed so that you can complete it from the recording alone. If you miss three or more sessions in a row, a member of the support team will check in to see if you want to defer to the next cohort. Deferring is free on Pro Annual. On the Student plan, you can defer once per purchase.
