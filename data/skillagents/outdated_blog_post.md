# Announcing our 30-day Full Refund Guarantee

Posted on January 15, 2024 by the SkillAgents AI team

Today is a big day at SkillAgents AI. Starting right now, every paid plan on skillagents.ai comes with a 30-day full refund guarantee. No questions asked. No processing fee. No fine print.

Here is how it works. You sign up for any paid plan, whether that is Student, Pro Annual, or Team. You get thirty days from the date of purchase to try everything the plan offers. If, at any point in those thirty days, you decide the course is not for you, you request a refund and you get every rupee or every dollar back. The full amount. We do not deduct a processing fee. We do not pro-rate. We do not ask you to justify the decision.

Why are we doing this? Two reasons.

The first reason is trust. When you pay for a course from a small company like ours, you are taking a bet on a person you probably have not met. We want you to feel safe placing that bet. A clean refund policy says "we think the course is good, and we are willing to put our money where our mouth is".

The second reason is focus. A great refund policy forces us to build a great product. If we know that an unhappy student can walk away with a full refund on day twenty-nine, we think twice before cutting corners on week four. The policy keeps us honest.

A few quick notes on how to use it. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method. You can request a refund by replying to your purchase receipt or by writing to support@skillagents.ai with your order ID. We aim to process every request within two business days. Team plan refunds follow the same rules as individual plans, calculated on the purchase date of the team seat.

This policy is effective immediately for all new and existing customers. If you bought a plan in the last thirty days and you want out, write to us and we will make it right.
