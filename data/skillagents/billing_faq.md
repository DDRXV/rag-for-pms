# SkillAgents AI Billing FAQ

This page answers the questions the billing team receives most often. If your question is not here, email support@skillagents.ai with your order ID.

## How do I update my credit card?

Sign in to skillagents.ai, go to Settings, Billing, Payment methods. Click "Replace card" and enter the new details. The new card is charged on your next renewal. If a recent renewal failed because of an expired card, the system retries within two hours of the update.

## How do I get a proper tax invoice for my employer?

Every payment generates a tax invoice in PDF form. You will find it under Settings, Billing, Invoices. Each invoice includes the SkillAgents AI GSTIN, your billing address, the plan purchased, and the tax breakdown. If you need the billing name changed to your employer's legal name, send the request to support@skillagents.ai before you pay so the first invoice is correct. Corrections after the fact are possible but take up to five business days.

## Why did my payment fail?

The most common reason is error code E-4012, which means your bank declined the charge. Banks decline for many reasons: insufficient funds, international transaction blocks, or fraud checks on a new merchant. The fix is to call your bank, ask them to approve SkillAgents AI as a merchant, and retry the payment. If the retry still fails, try a different card.

## Can I switch from Student to Pro Annual?

Yes. From Settings, Billing, Plan, pick "Upgrade to Pro Annual". You pay the difference between $499 and $1,499, which is $1,000. Your cohort access does not reset. Everything you have completed so far carries into the Pro Annual plan.

## Do you accept purchase orders?

Purchase orders are accepted on the Team and Enterprise plans only. Send the PO to sales@skillagents.ai with your company details and the number of seats you need. Net-30 terms are standard. Free Trial, Student, and Pro Annual are self-serve only and do not support purchase orders.

## How does annual billing work?

Pro Annual and Team renew on the anniversary of your purchase. Ten days before renewal, you receive an email reminder with the amount and date. To avoid the charge, cancel from the billing page before that date.

## Is GST included in the listed price?

For customers billed in India, GST at 18% is included in the listed price. Your invoice shows the base amount and the GST component separately. For customers outside India, prices are exclusive of local taxes, and your invoice reflects that.

## Can I pause my subscription?

Pro Annual subscriptions cannot be paused, but you can defer your cohort participation to a later month at no cost. Team and Enterprise pause requests are handled case by case. Write to support@skillagents.ai with the dates you want to pause and the reason.

## What currencies do you charge in?

Payments are in US dollars for customers outside India and in Indian rupees for customers billed in India.
