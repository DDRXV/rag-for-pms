# SkillAgents AI Refund Quick Answers

This page is a short community reference written by the student success team. It routes common refund questions to the right contact. It does not contain any refund numbers, percentages, fees, or calculations. For the exact amounts and the math, always open the official refund policy document.

## Who handles refund requests?

The billing team at support@skillagents.ai handles every refund request on every plan. Email them with your order ID from the original receipt and they respond within two business days. They can also escalate complex cases to the finance team if needed.

## Is there a refund option for paid customers?

Yes, every paid customer on SkillAgents AI is eligible for some form of refund. The specific terms, timelines, and any deductions are documented in the official refund policy. The student success team cannot quote refund amounts over email. They will always point you back at the official policy for the numbers.

## Where can I read the full refund terms?

The official refund policy document is the single source of truth. Open the refund policy PDF from the support portal or ask the student success team to send it to you. Any community post, blog post, or forum reply that contradicts the official policy is out of date and should be ignored.

## Who can help me understand my specific order?

For questions about your specific order, email support@skillagents.ai with your order ID. The student success team can walk you through which clauses apply to your purchase. For general questions about refund eligibility, the same inbox is the fastest route to a human.

## Do you process refunds back to the original card?

Yes, every approved refund lands back on the original payment method within seven to ten business days. For the exact refund amount you can expect on your order, read the official refund policy before you open a support ticket.
